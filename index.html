Fire-Fighting Robot

Team Size: 3
Mentor: Dr. Abhilasha Joshi

We designed and built a prototype fire-fighting robot controlled via a Bluetooth module. The robot is equipped with temperature, flame, and smoke sensors that help detect fire in the surrounding area. Once fire is detected, the robot can be manually operated to move closer to the fire. A water pump installed on the robot is then used to spray water to control or extinguish the fire. To improve safety and visibility, we also added a camera module that provides a live view of areas that are too dangerous for humans to enter. This project combined both hardware and sensor integration to create a functional and cost-effective solution for fire emergencies.
